About The Blueprint

The Blueprint is a self-published magazine (zine) that showcases the interests of NYU Tandon students in the arts and sciences.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Blueprint?

The Blueprint is an outlet for students in the Tandon campus to present their creative works, whether it may pertain to literary, audio and visual works.

How do I submit?

Students can submit via the Google Forms submission page that can be accessed through the button on this page.

What can I submit?

Students can submit anything ranging from visual media, creative writing, music, etc.
